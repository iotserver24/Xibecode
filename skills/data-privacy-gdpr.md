# Data Privacy & Compliance

## Minimization

- Collect only data you need; document retention and deletion paths.

## Storage

- Encrypt sensitive data at rest and in transit when the stack requires it; never log raw PII unnecessarily.

## User rights

- If the product serves EU users, align export/delete flows with legal/product requirements.
